Case Study | Primrose Photography

Primrose Photography is a website showcasing the stunning portfolio of fictional photographer Primrose Rosenthal. The site offers visitors a chance to explore the portfolio and learn about various photography services.

The Problem

Primrose, a passionate photographer, had gained experience through various portrait projects for friends, family, and local businesses. She was ready to transition her hobby into a full-fledged photography business. The primary challenge was to create a professional online presence that showcased her work and attracted potential clients.

Designing the Start Process

The first step in this project was to identify the target audience Primrose aimed to attract. By conducting research and gathering insights on potential clients, we tailored the website’s design and content to appeal to her desired demographic. The website was styled according to Primrose’s preferences, featuring a curated selection of her best work. This approach allowed visitors to easily browse through her portfolio, gaining a clear understanding of her photography style and services.

Research & Insights

To gain a deeper understanding of the target audience, a survey was conducted to explore user preferences for photography on websites and social media. A diverse participant pool was assembled, including a younger demographic with a strong social media presence. This approach yielded insights primarily from individuals aged 16-19 with interests in cosplay, gaming, and other creative pursuits.

Survey results indicated a strong preference for portrait photography, particularly among those involved in cosplay. Young users often seek photographers who capture life milestones, such as school dances and graduations. Based on these findings, a user persona representing a young, socially active individual was developed to guide the website's design.

In terms of website design, participants favored a balance of simplicity and complexity while prioritizing clear organization. Common frustrations with other websites included clutter and poorly functioning elements. These insights informed design decisions to create a user-friendly and visually appealing platform.

By applying these research findings, the website was tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of the target audience, ultimately establishing Primrose for success in the photography market.

Overview of Site Content & Structure

  • The Landing Page highlighted Primrose’s top photography pieces and the services she offered as a professional photographer.
  • The Main Gallery displayed a variety of her work, including portraits of friends, family, and product photography for small businesses.
  • The Single Style Gallery focused on her specialty in portrait photography, accompanied by her reflections on selected photoshoots.
  • The About the Photographer page provided an introduction to Primrose, with a section called “Photography Gear” to showcase the equipment she used.
  • The Contact Page featured a form for potential clients to get in touch and book a photoshoot.

Branding & Style Guide

Color Palette

The soft, earthy tones reflect Primrose Photography’s natural and serene aesthetic. Muted greens and yellows evoke a nostalgic, vintage feel, adding warmth and cohesion across the site.

Typography

A blend of decorative serif for titles and clean sans-serif for body text balances elegance with readability, reflecting the brand’s artistic flair while ensuring a user-friendly experience.

UX/UI Components

Logos, icons, and buttons are designed for both aesthetics and functionality, providing clear visual feedback and consistency. Images showcase Primrose’s photography range, and content tags improve navigation by highlighting key areas of expertise.

These design choices collectively enhance the brand’s identity, creating a visually appealing and intuitive user experience that resonates with Primrose’s target audience.

The Final Design

The final design for Primrose Photography blends a soft, earthy color palette with classic serif typography to convey warmth and professionalism. The UX components, including intuitive navigation and responsive layouts, ensure an optimal browsing experience across devices. This cohesive visual identity highlights Primrose's artistry, making her portfolio easily accessible to potential clients while reinforcing her brand's unique aesthetic.
